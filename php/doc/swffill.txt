SWFFill -- Loads SWFFill object

  SWFFill swffill()~

The |swffill| object allows you to transform (scale, skew, rotate) bitmap and
gradient fills. |swffill| objects are created by the |swfshape->addfill|
methods.

SWFFill has the following methods: |swffill->moveto| and |swffill->scaleto|,
|swffill->rotateto|, |swffill->skewxto| and |swffill->skewyto|.
